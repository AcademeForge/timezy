What you get

Everything you need
to manage your time.

Timezy is built around three screens — Home, Tasks, and Planner — so your entire schedule lives in one place.

🏠

Daily Dashboard

See today's tasks, daily progress, and upcoming deadlines the moment you open the app. No digging required.

✅

Task Manager

Create, edit, and complete tasks with priority labels (High, Medium, Low), due dates, and descriptions.

📅

Visual Planner

Browse tasks by calendar day. Colour-coded dots on the calendar show exactly which days have pending or completed work.

🔥

Progress Ring

A live streak ring on the home screen shows today's completion percentage so you always know where you stand.

🔍

Search & Filter

Filter tasks by status or priority, and full-text search across titles and descriptions instantly.

🌗

Dark & Light Mode

Timezy looks great in both themes and remembers your preference across sessions automatically.

How it works

Three steps.
One clear day.

1

Add your tasks

Create tasks with a title, optional description, priority level, and a due date. Takes under 10 seconds.

2

Plan your week

Open the Planner and tap any day to see what's due. Use the calendar view to spot busy and free days at a glance.

3

Track and complete

Mark tasks done from any screen. Watch your daily progress ring fill up as you move through the day.
